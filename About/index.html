The Engineer Behind the Keyboards

Where precision electronics engineering meets bespoke mechanical hardware tuning.

I started KeebForge with a straightforward goal: to push the boundaries of mechanical keyboards beyond off-the-shelf limits. As an Electronics Engineer, I don't look at a keyboard simply as a workspace device—I treat it as an intricate collection of structural physics, audio performance variables, and circuit timing layers.

Operating out of Jammu & Kashmir, I run an advanced engineering workshop designed specifically for mail-in micro-soldering, structural diagnostic tuning, and detailed custom builds. Every job that passes over my workbench is handled with deep attention to detail, clean flux management, and uniform lubrication.

Precision Craftsmanship

Every single switch is systematically opened, inspected, and lubricated manually with clean, even layers of premium Krytox grease. No shortcuts, no over-lubing—just pure, fluid keystrokes.

Circuit Level Expertise

From designing standalone custom layouts and routing multi-layer PCBs to troubleshooting trace damage and fixing torn pads, your keyboard's electronics are handled safely with industrial equipment.

Whether you are a casual typist seeking a pristine sound profile, a software engineer trying to find an ergonomic split layout, or a competitive gamer looking for specialized mod adjustments, my workshop is engineered to realize your exact hardware specifications.
